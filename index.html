About Me

I was raised in Santa Cruz and recently relocated to Humboldt from Orange County, where I had the opportunity to work at several esteemed salons. Growing up with a hairstylist mother, I developed a deep passion for hair and beauty from an early age. I specialize in hair color, offering beautiful, polished blowouts and vibrant, colorful extensions. I am thrilled to now be part of the Humboldt community and to share my craft here. When I’m not in the salon, you can often find me practicing yoga, foraging in the redwoods, painting, or enjoying live music with friends.
